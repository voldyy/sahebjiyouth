Purpose & Community

Our Mission.

Sahebji Youth represents the youth worldwide affiliated with Anoopam Mission, a socio-spiritual, global, non-profit organization within the Hindu-Swaminarayan faith.

Taking inspiration and guidance from our guru, Sant Bhagwant Sahebji, we incorporate traditional Swaminarayan values into our lives and strive to be positive, constructive influences within our families, communities, and society as a whole.

Guruhari Sahebdada loves the youth unconditionally, and this love has inspired us to always seek the divinity in every circumstance. Sahebji Youth live by his saying:

Think Positive and the Rest Will Follow

Core Values

Inclusiveness

We embrace diversity and strive to bring everyone together with the goal of serving God by serving humanity. We provide a positive and inclusive environment where youth feel comfortable sharing their opinions and expressing themselves freely.

Integrity

We uphold the values and principles set forth by our Hindu culture. We strive for transparency in our socio-religious philosophies and believe in the importance of righteous living and being a positive influence on future generations.

Leadership

We are deeply committed to the idea of “leading by example.” We focus on fostering this philosophy, which has been demonstrated to us by our guru, Sahebji. We strive to cultivate leadership qualities within our youth.

Experience

About Us.

Sahebji Youth hosts youth events worldwide, including youth camps, leadership retreats, youth conventions, and shibirs. These events offer a one-of-a-kind experience where youth can build lifelong friendships through collaborative learning, friendly competitions, stimulating workshops, and spiritual growth.

Youth from across the world come together to participate in daily inspirational discourses, group discussions, and engaging activities tailored to their age group, deepening their understanding of our culture and religion. And this is all designed to create divine memories and lasting connections.
