Yogi Youth Camp

YYC offers a one-of-a-kind experience where campers can build lifelong friendships through collaborative learning, friendly competitions, stimulating workshops, and spiritual growth. Youth from across the nation come together to participate in daily inspirational discourses, group discussions, and engaging activities tailored to their age group, deepening their understanding of our culture and religion.

Get ready for exciting evening events, creative arts and crafts, and thrilling sports competitions—all designed to create divine memories and lasting connections.

June 21 - 27, 2026 Coplay, PA

Registration

Check-in for YYC will open on Sunday, June 21, 2026, at 1:00 PM. Upon arrival, please proceed with your luggage to the designated check-in area, where volunteers will be ready to assist you.

  1. Meet Volunteers – A team of volunteers will greet you and guide you through the check-in process.
  2. Provide Your Name – Check in by giving your name to the registration team.
  3. Donations – If you wish to make an additional donation, you will have the opportunity to do so at check-in.
  4. Luggage Tagging – Volunteers will help you tag your luggage to ensure it is properly stored.
  5. Join the Campers – Once checked in, you can join the rest of the campers, mingle, and settle in before the opening ceremony.

Please plan to depart our facilities by 1:00 PM on Saturday, June 27, 2026. Ensure that all personal belongings are collected and all check-out procedures are completed before your departure.

We look forward to welcoming you and wish you a great time at YYC!

Fee

We request that each camper pay an application fee of $100 to reserve their spot as an attendee. This can be paid via credit or debit card on the last page of the registration form. Please note, this fee is non-refundable and does not qualify as tax-deductible. Any additional donations are warmly welcomed and can be made via Paypal Giving Fund or in-person during the check-in process on June 21.

Parental Supervision Policy

Children between the ages of 5-8 will be considered freshman campers but must be accompanied by a parent or guardian. There are no organized activities for children ages 4 and under. If a parent wishes to bring a child age 4 or under, they must remain with them at all times. Parents are not required to complete a registration form. For further information or inquiries, please email info@sahebjiyouth.org.

Transportation

Airport pick-ups will be available from the following airports: Allentown (ABE), Newark (EWR), Philadelphia (PHL), and Trenton (TTN). We kindly ask campers to be mindful when booking early morning or late-night flights out of respect for our dedicated volunteers coordinating transportation. Planning your arrival and departure at reasonable times will help ensure a smooth and efficient travel experience for everyone.

Accommodations

All YYC campers will be comfortably housed in nearby hotels in the Allentown area, with separate accommodations for boys and girls. Daily camp activities will take place on the Mandir campus, where an enriching and immersive experience awaits. Transportation will be provided between the hotels and the Mandir to ensure a smooth and convenient stay.

Local campers have the option to commute from home, but we kindly request that you arrive on time each morning according to the provided schedule to fully participate in the camp experience.

Groups

To create a more engaging and enriching experience, campers will be grouped by age for select activities. These include inspiring sabhas, dynamic team-building exercises, and thoughtful discussion groups, ensuring meaningful connections and age-appropriate learning. Each group will have the opportunity to bond, grow, and immerse themselves in the YYC experience alongside peers.

Facilities

We offer a range of facilities to enhance your experience, including:

  • A large, air-conditioned temple hall for gatherings and programs.
  • A spacious, air-conditioned dining hall for meals.
  • A field for soccer, volleyball, and group games.
  • A college regulation outdoor basketball court for recreational play.

Code of Conduct Agreement

As an attendee of the Yogi Youth Camp, I agree to:

  1. Treat all participants, instructors, and volunteers with respect, courtesy, and cooperation.
  2. Avoid actions that could harm others physically or emotionally. Fighting, bullying, or other inappropriate behaviors are strictly prohibited and may result in dismissal without refund.
